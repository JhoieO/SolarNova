Solar PV System Installation

Our installations are typically solar PV (photovoltaic) systems. These are systems which are common throughout the UK and are now Africa has the opportunity to harness the sun’s energy to save on electricity bills.

Consultancy

Solar PV Water Plants

Ground-mount Solar systems are ideal if you have ample property space within a back garden or farmland. It is an excellent alternative to a roof-mounted system and solar water pumps convert solar power from the sun into electrical power to run a water distribution pump.

Read More

SV Batteries/Inverters

We use only the best products in the solar industry. Our inverters come with Wifi capability to monitor system performance on desktops, tablets or smartphone devices. The inverter changes the DC electricity (collected from the solar panels) to AC electricity so that it can be used in the home.

Read More

Community Energy

As a social enterprise, working on community energy projects across Africa is an integral part of what we do. We support communities in shaping the energy transition. We spearheaded Kenyan’s only community solar water pump farm and we are excellently placed to support more communities in becoming renewable energy generators that supply and sell power.

Trainings and Education

Since the company's inception, events are organised and trainings delivered to communities, SME’s, energy professionals and citizens across Africa. We try to trainings and events that encourages nature based solutions.
